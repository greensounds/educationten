Methodology

Education TEN stands for Tracking Education with News (T.E.N.). Each week 24 news mediums are consulted for articles focusing on education. Each article is read, categorized, itemized and graphed. With this, an overview on what is trending in the news (with regard to education) and which States are “on the map” is provided.
