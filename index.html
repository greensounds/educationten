Education TEN

TEN stands for Tracking Education with News (T.E.N.). Each week, both American and Mexican, news providers are consulted for articles focusing on education. Each article is read, categorized, itemized and graphed. With this, an overview on what is trending in the news, with regard to education, and which States are “on the map” is provided.

With regard to the United States, the tracking venture began on October 26, 2015. A total of 24 news suppliers are consulted on a daily basis. So far 988 news articles have been curated.

For Mexico, the scouting began January 11, 2016. A total of 21 news suppliers are consulted on a daily basis; so far 105 news articles have been curated.
